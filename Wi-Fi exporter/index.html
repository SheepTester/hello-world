# Wi-Fi password exporter On android at least you can go to settings > network and Internet . internet . saved networks to see a list of all wifi networks you've connected to. i dont know if wifi passwords get backed up anywhere so just in case, this allows you to somewhat easily (though still painstakingly) export each wifi network s password. 1. click "start" 1.settings > network and Internet . internet . go to saved networks 2. tap each network, 3. click on the share button 4. scan fingerprint 5. Show QR code to the webcam until you hear a ding 6. repeat with all the networks the network's are saved locally so you won't lose them if you accidentally close the browser. once you're done, click the download as CSV button and be happy
